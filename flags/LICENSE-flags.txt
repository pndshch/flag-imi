Flag SVG files in this directory
================================

Source: hampusborgos/country-flags
        https://github.com/hampusborgos/country-flags

Files are named by ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 country code (e.g. jp.svg), with
xk.svg used for Kosovo.

National flag designs are in the public domain — they are not protected
by copyright, and the source repository states the same. These SVG files
are redistributed here on that basis.

Note: some countries place separate legal rules on how their flag may be
displayed or used. Those rules are independent of copyright status and are
not affected by the public-domain nature of the design.
